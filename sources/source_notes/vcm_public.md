## Thesis

VCM treats the context window as a compiled working cache over a larger versioned semantic address space. Its affirmative claim is control-plane correctness and observability, not general model-accuracy superiority.

## Mechanisms

- Semantic objects, immutable content versions, representations, validations, packets, and materializations.
- Source and authority binding, role-indexed obligations, adequacy/admission separation, protected compilation, snapshot coherence, revocation, invalidation, and audit records.
- Explicit separation among control, evidence, quotation, data, action, processing exposure, output influence, and authorization.
- Typed non-success states including unknown adequacy, deferred admission, proven infeasibility, feasibility unknown, ask, abstain, deny, and retry.

## Evidence

- The source describes executable conformance artifacts and bounded mechanism studies.
- It explicitly preserves a negative/non-result for a small model-facing packet-format pilot.
- It does not establish end-to-end superiority on frozen pretrained models, natural histories, or all neighboring systems.

## Failure Modes

- Summaries self-promoting into evidence or authority.
- Hostile quoted text becoming behavioral control.
- Stale, revoked, or infeasible context being silently admitted.
- Future-answer leakage, over-taint, and semantic heuristics with low recall.

## Claims To Add Or Update

- Use VCM as the governed memory/context layer.
- Use VCM to surface memory/context failure modes: hidden authority, stale or revoked context, taint leakage, future-answer leakage, deferred admission, and unrecorded adequacy gaps.
- Use VCM in cyclic-memory chapters only for addressability, taint, versioning, revocation, adequacy, and certificate boundaries; do not use it as evidence for recurrence quality.
- Use VCM to require fast-generation routes to name context packets, authority, taint, revocation, and adequacy boundaries before measuring speed.
- Use VCM to define context-policy rewards around source binding, adequacy/admission separation, taint, revocation, and non-success states rather than retrieval volume alone.
- Keep VCM evidence claims bounded to control-plane properties unless external or local tests are recorded.
- Formalize adequacy/admission separation, authority non-escalation, taint, and snapshot validity as proof/schema targets.
