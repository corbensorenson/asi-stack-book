## Thesis

Croissant is a comparator for JSON-LD dataset structure, resources, checksums, record fields, machine-readable provenance, usage conditions, and ML-tool portability.

## Mechanisms

- The inventory records structured dataset metadata, resource references, checksums, provenance, and usage-condition surfaces.
- It is routed to supply-chain, artifact, benchmark, and continual-learning data discussions.

## Evidence

- This note records the specification’s comparator role only.
- Metadata conformance does not establish data integrity, fitness, legality, representativeness, or safe use.

## Failure Modes

- Mistaking a checksum or declared field for complete lineage or dataset quality.
- Treating a metadata schema as evidence of lawful or safe training use.
