## Thesis

The Coherence Exchange proposes an epistemic-liquidity layer where claims or knowledge units become tradeable, reviewable, and governable through verification supply chains, value/accounting mechanisms, and rights such as fork, exit, and audit.

## Mechanisms

- Verified Epistemic Units as structured claim/evidence objects.
- Verification supply chain connecting PlanForge, Spinoza, tribunals, and audit.
- Intelligence arbitrage and coherence/liquidity framing for moving knowledge between agents, tools, or institutions.
- Governance concepts including fork, exit, audit, and contestability.
