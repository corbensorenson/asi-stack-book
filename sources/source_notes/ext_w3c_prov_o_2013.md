## Thesis

PROV-O is a vocabulary comparator for interoperable asserted provenance over entities, activities, agents, derivation, attribution, delegation, revision, and invalidation.

## Mechanisms

- The inventory identifies the ontology’s entity/activity/agent and provenance-relation vocabulary.
- It is routed to claim, artifact, supply-chain, and data-lifecycle provenance discussions.

## Evidence

- The note records a standard-level comparator, not a local conformance or integrity result.
- An asserted provenance graph does not by itself establish truth, completeness, integrity, authority, or safety.

## Failure Modes

- Treating recorded derivation as verified derivation.
- Treating interoperability vocabulary as an authorization or tamper-resistance control.
