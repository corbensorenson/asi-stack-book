## Adequacy verdict

Adequate for one finite authored allocation-to-claim-transport lifecycle and exact bounded result identity. Inadequate for economic optimality, calibrated prices or uncertainty, useful throughput, real reviewer/verifier capacity, queue stability, serving behavior, model quality, safety, deployed scheduling, simulation adequacy, physical feasibility, independent reproduction, transfer, or SOTA. Authored cost, capacity, verifier, useful-outcome, fidelity, transfer, recovery, and opportunity fields are not measurements. No support transition or external effect follows.
