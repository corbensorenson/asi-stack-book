The lifecycle binds exact task, context, mode, matched baseline, verifier, result, and residual identities across eight stages. ## Adequacy verdict

Adequate for one finite authored context-to-closure policy and exact bounded result identity. Inadequate for model generation, actual verifier independence, timing rigor, natural-workload breadth, strong matched-model comparison, useful-throughput generalization, deployed fallback, safety, reproduction, transfer, or SOTA. Authored `taskSuccess`, `usefulDenominator`, and fallback fields are not measurements. No support transition or external effect follows.
