## Scope

This local result covers synthetic living-book change-packet records only. It
checks packet identity, public-surface changelog refs, validation-command
receipts, render-command receipts, reader/audio derivative authority
boundaries, support-state transition refs, residuals for blocked or incomplete
packets, and explicit non-claims.

## Non-Claims

- Does not approve a release, reader artifact, EPUB, PDF, DOCX, audio artifact,
  source interpretation, manuscript quality, editorial quality, live-site
  availability, or support-state promotion.
- Does not prove that future agents will maintain the book correctly.
