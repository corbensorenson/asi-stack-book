## Reader Promise

A human reader should leave this chapter understanding that a serious AI system
needs version control for truth claims. Prose can communicate claims, but the
ledger gives each material claim identity, support state, provenance,
contradiction links, uncertainty, revision history, and publication boundaries.
Belief revision is presented as controlled state change rather than as an
embarrassing editorial correction.
