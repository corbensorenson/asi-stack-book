## Reader Promise

This pass should let a human reader understand why compact context is safe to
reuse only when the representation keeps provenance, loss, authority,
permitted use, validity, revocation state, and residual risk attached.

The reader throughline is:

> a summary is not reusable context until it carries its loss, provenance,
> authority, permitted use, and revocation state with it.
