Last updated: 2026-07-13

mixed evidence dispositions, and nine-owner book reconciliation.

## Claim And Review Boundary

All 54 chapter-core claims remain at `argument`. The release records five exact
non-core mechanism findings as bounded promote recommendations for later
ordinary evidence review, two narrowings, two exact-corpus refutations, one
no-change transfer boundary, and eight QCSA residuals. It does not claim
matched-resource advantage, active-question value beyond the exact corpus,
natural-task or learned-model transfer, independent evaluator validity,
production rollback, safety, privacy, security, universal grounding, AGI, or
ASI capability. No external-human prepublication review was requested or
claimed.

PDF, AZW3, MOBI, MP3, M4B, audio-embedded EPUB, and a separate curated-reader
