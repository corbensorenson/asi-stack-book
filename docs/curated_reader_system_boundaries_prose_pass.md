A human reader should leave this chapter understanding that authority in the
ASI Stack is the difference between "can" and "may": a typed transition record
that names who is asking, what boundary they want to cross, which grant and
ceiling apply, how denial or escalation is recorded, and why a capable system
must still be able to stop.
