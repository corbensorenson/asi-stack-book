# v2.2.0 Release Scope

Last updated: 2026-07-11

The v2.2.0 residual-and-transfer release is the canonical live/research HTML book only.
It preserves the title and 54-chapter architecture while adding the
completed post-v2.1 empirical programs, conservative dispositions, six modern
external-source routes, and the Question-Compiled Semantic Addressing successor
synthesis across nine existing chapter owners.

## Included

- the exact Quarto HTML site built from the tagged source commit;
- AI and Human reading modes over the same 54 active chapters;
- all public-safe P1–P3 preregistration, model-call, evaluator, state-tree,
  outcome, negative-result, residual, and reconciliation artifacts;
- QCSA's digest-bound public-safe source record, source note, passage-reviewed
  chapter mappings, and cross-chapter design-rationale integration, while the
  complete raw author paper remains in the local raw-source cache;
- the updated source, claim, evidence-vector, proof, test, rights, status, and
  roadmap surfaces generated from the same manifest state;
- one content-addressed GitHub Release archive for `v2.2.0`, published only
  after public redownload and SHA-256 verification; and
- mutable root and `/latest/` deployment from the same clean tested bundle,
  without representing either URL as immutable storage.

## Claim and review boundary

All 54 chapter-core claims remain at `argument`. The release publishes three
bounded local empirical programs, fourteen current core-claim `no_change`
decisions, six claim-bounded non-core transitions, eleven residual
dispositions, and one Corben-authored conceptual source synthesis. It does not
claim population or production transfer, generated-answer utility, external
institutional independence, verifier correctness, production rollback,
influence/privacy/storage erasure, QCSA implementation or performance,
deployment safety, AGI, or ASI capability. No external-human prepublication
review was requested or claimed.

## Selected formats

The canonical live/research HTML book is the only selected format. EPUB, DOCX,
PDF, AZW3, MOBI, MP3, M4B, audio-embedded EPUB, and a separate curated-reader
release remain optional future products and are not v2.2.0 artifacts.

## Rights decision

The internal ownership/provenance review retains the split-license model:
paths routed `CC-BY-4.0` or `Apache-2.0` by
`licensing/final_release_rights_routing.json` receive only that exact-tag grant;
all `excluded-no-grant` paths remain reserved. Sources, experiments, raw model
outputs, generated candidates, checkpoints, result bundles, historical reader
derivatives, and external-review records remain excluded. Official license
texts are not self-relicensed. This internal review is not legal advice,
third-party clearance, or a grant of trademark, endorsement, privacy,
publicity, or unlisted patent rights.
