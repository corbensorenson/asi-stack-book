A human reader should leave this chapter understanding that an AI capability
upgrade is not accepted merely because a candidate looks better. It becomes a
replacement only after a transaction preserves field identity, regression
floors, authority limits, evaluator independence, residual ownership, monitor
evidence, and rollback readiness.
