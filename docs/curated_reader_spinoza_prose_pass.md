## Reader Promise

useful only when it narrows the claim, records the interpretation mapping,
keeps artifact validity separate from claim validity, and routes failure to
downgrade, block, residual, or review.

The reader throughline is:

> a proof artifact earns trust by narrowing the claim it touches.
