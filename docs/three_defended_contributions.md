# Three Defended Contributions

Last updated: 2026-07-10

The book remains broad, but the research program now permits exactly three
program-level defended contributions. The older five-track portfolio remains
useful as subordinate work packages; it is no longer permission to present five
independent flagship claims, much less one flagship claim per chapter.

## Governed-cognition interface contracts

The defended target is the join between responsibility, authority, context,
planning, verification, execution, replacement, and review: typed contracts
should preserve refusal, narrowing, rollback, and accountable handoff state.
The governed repository-change slice and authority/revocation trace invariants
are the shared executable lane. This is not a claim of complete ASI, deployed
enforcement, alignment, safety, or optimal modularity.

Primary owners are System Boundaries and Authority, Intent-to-Execution
Contracts, Recursive Self-Improvement Boundaries, and Integrated Reference
Architecture. The other assigned chapters supply mechanisms or integration;
they are not separate flagship contributions.

## Public claim-state transition discipline

The defended target is a living technical work that makes claim identity,
support movement, negative evidence, review, scope, and prohibited inference
explicit enough to block silent promotion and evidence laundering. The shared
lane is the claim/evidence ledger, transition gates, contradiction controls,
and adversarial counterexample pressure testing. This does not prove the ladder is
epistemically optimal, that reviewers are correct, or that any chapter thesis
is true.

Primary owners are Evidence States and Claim Discipline, Claim Ledgers and
Belief Revision, Executable Specifications and the Lean Proof Envelope, and
Living Book Methodology.

## Record/reality reconciliation and residual honesty

The defended target is that receipts and ledgers become useful governance only
when independent observation can challenge them and unresolved effects, costs,
failures, and rollback gaps remain conserved as owned residuals. The shared
lane is the matched governed/baseline workload, receipt mismatch, hidden
residual, rollback, and governance-overhead measurement. This does not prove
complete observation, tamper-proof logging, safe governance, efficient
oversight, or production reliability.

Primary owners are Artifact Graphs, Compact Generative Systems and Residual
Honesty, and Resource Economics and Token Budgets.

## Chapter assignment rule

`products/contribution_focus_contract.json` assigns all 61 manifest chapters
to exactly one primary program contribution. Eleven are primary owners; the
other 50 are supporting or integration chapters. Every assignment explicitly
sets `independent_flagship_claim` to false. The contract is generated in
manifest order and checked by `scripts/validate_contribution_focus_contract.py`.

The map is a scope-control mechanism, not novelty proof, evidence, external
review, or support-state movement. A primary owner can still fail its
falsification target, be narrowed by prior art, or remain at `argument`.
