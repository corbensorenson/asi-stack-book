# Alignment Metaphysics Boundary Audit

Last updated: 2026-07-02

This audit keeps the Constitutional Alignment chapter from laundering
metaphysical or philosophical source lineage into technical proof, enforcement
authority, or support-state movement.

## Scope

The audit checks the live chapter, curated reader chapter, selected source
notes, the outline, roadmap, changelog, manifest, and book validation wiring.
It verifies that:

- metaphysical and consciousness language is labeled as lineage,
  interpretation, speculative context, or design rationale;
- external Constitutional AI and corrigibility sources remain comparator-only;
- the reader chapter does not ask readers to accept a full moral theory before
  accepting the engineering move;
- source notes preserve the distinction between author-intent context and
  empirical evidence;
- all support-state effects remain `none`.

## Boundary

This audit is a text and surface check only. It does not prove metaphysical
claims, consciousness claims, moral correctness, deployed constitutional
alignment, runtime corrigibility, reviewer quality, approval-service quality,
source truth, source interpretation adequacy, rights usability, or support-state
promotion. In short: no support-state transition.
