adjacent checks as substitutes for evidence. It selected one bounded natural-
work or end-to-end bundle per family, replays every selected validator, binds
the exact result digest, retains each bundle's negative controls, and states

## Why the P5 gate can close

P5 requires two different things. First, every material atom must receive an
exact treatment or terminal full-attempt gap. The terminal-disposition audit
provides that at a 3,745-atom denominator. Second, every family must have at
least one competent natural-work or end-to-end bundle with relevant negative
controls. The eight bundles above provide that minimum breadth.

Closing P5 does not erase the residual ledger. The 3,698 blocked atoms remain
the book's explicit research burden, and no single family bundle proves all
atoms assigned to that family. Those gaps constrain P6 comparisons and must be
reflected during P7 chapter reconciliation.

## Rejecting controls and authority

The coverage validator rejects family deletion, validator erasure, digest
rewriting, invented chapter-core promotion, and blocked-gap erasure. It reruns
all eight selected validators on every audit pass. Publication and release
authority remain `none`.
