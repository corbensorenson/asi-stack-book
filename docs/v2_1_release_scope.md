# v2.1.0 Release Scope

Last updated: 2026-07-10

The v2.1.0 evidence release is the canonical live/research HTML book only. It
keeps the v2 title and 54-chapter architecture while adding the three completed
post-v2 empirical programs, nine existing-chapter updates, accepted no-change
decisions, evidence vectors, residuals, and exact public-safe result artifacts.

## Included

- the exact Quarto HTML site built from the tagged source commit;
- AI and Human reading modes over the same 54 active chapters;
- the public-safe governed-work, routing/deliberation, and update-causality
  reports, results, validators, checkpoints, raw model outputs, negative
  results, and no-change records;
- narrative, architecture-reference, evidence-registry, source, proof, test,
  status, license, and release surfaces linked from the site;
- one content-addressed GitHub Release archive for `v2.1.0`, redownloaded and
  SHA-256 verified; and
- mutable root and `/latest/` deployment from the same tested commit-bound
  bundle without being represented as immutable storage.

## Claim and review boundary

All 54 chapter-core claims remain at `argument`. The release publishes three
bounded local empirical packets and nine `no_change` decisions; it does not
claim production transfer, external institutional independence, verifier
correctness, production unlearning, open-ended or recursive improvement,
deployment safety, AGI, or ASI capability. No external-human prepublication
review was requested or claimed.

## Selected formats

The canonical live/research HTML book is the only selected format. EPUB, DOCX,
PDF, AZW3, MOBI, MP3, M4B, audio-embedded EPUB, and a separate curated-reader
release remain optional future products and are not v2.1.0 artifacts.

## Rights decision

The internal ownership/provenance review retains the v2 split-license model:
paths routed `CC-BY-4.0` or `Apache-2.0` by
`licensing/final_release_rights_routing.json` receive only that exact-tag grant;
all `excluded-no-grant` paths remain reserved. Experiment directories,
including raw model outputs, generated candidates, checkpoint artifacts, and
result bundles, remain excluded from the outbound grant. Official license
texts are not self-relicensed. This internal review is not legal advice,
third-party clearance, or a grant of trademark, endorsement, privacy,
publicity, or unlisted patent rights.
