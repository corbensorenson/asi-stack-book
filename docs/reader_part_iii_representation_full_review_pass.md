## Review Criteria

The generated reader text was read end to end for:

- continuity from artifact compression into semantic structure, budget
  accounting, simulation fidelity, and optional substrate adoption;
- overlay application and coherence in Semantic Representation and Mathematical
  and Search Substrates;
- preservation of provenance, grounding, permitted-use, adequacy,
  interoperability, resource, fidelity, transfer, baseline, consumer-gate, and
  falsification boundaries;
- support-boundary preservation in each `Core Claim`;
- clear separation of design targets from implemented semantic graphs, budget
  schedulers, simulator results, substrate benchmarks, model-quality claims, and
  proof-status promotions;
- density that should become reader overlay, companion notes, or curated prose;
- handoff continuity into proof-carrying Circle contracts;
- absence of semantic-adequacy, resource-optimality, simulation-transfer,
  physical-feasibility, substrate-quality, benchmark, proof, runtime, or release
  overclaim.
