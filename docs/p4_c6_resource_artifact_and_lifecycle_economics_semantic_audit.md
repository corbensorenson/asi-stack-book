## The decisive semantic separations

is not open-world truth or complete provenance. A steward contract is not
competent or legitimate stewardship, and a contribution record is not
ownership. A reconstruction receipt or byte ratio is not useful compression or
semantic preservation. Finally, a closed record means only that declared
fields passed an authored finite route; it does not certify the real lifecycle.
